# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 072
**Title:** Quantum Disaster

## The Death (One Sentence)
A physics graduate student experimenting with a black-market quantum device accidentally shifts himself into a parallel timeline where he never existed, causing reality to violently reject his presence and erase him from existence.

## Tagline
"Some timelines aren't meant to have you in them."

## Themes
1. The arrogance of scientific tourism - treating dangerous experiments as adventures
2. The existential horror of non-existence
3. Playing with forces beyond human comprehension for the thrill of it

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 25%
- Horror: 55%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
STEM graduates who think their intelligence makes them invincible. Anyone who has casually talked about theoretical physics without understanding the implications. Travelers who seek out "weird science" experiences abroad.

## Comparison Titles
- "The Fly" meets "Coherence" in a backpacker hostel
- "Primer" meets "Final Destination"

## Content Warnings
- Existential horror and non-existence
- Body horror (reality rejection)
- Psychological distress
- Identity dissolution
- Pseudo-scientific concepts used for horror
